About the Firm

THDS Legal (Thakur Dinesh Singh & Associates), established in 1990, is a litigation-focused law firm with a distinguished presence in land acquisition matters, property disputes, civil litigation, and criminal defense.

The firm represents multiple government bodies including:

  • THDC-NTPC Thermal Power Plant
  • Bulandshahr Khurja Development Authority (BKDA)
  • Food Corporation of India (FCI)
  • and various other public authorities.

The firm represents clients from Trial Courts to the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India.

Practice Areas

I

Land Acquisition & Compensation Litigation

Specialized representation in land acquisition proceedings, compensation disputes, and rehabilitation matters before various forums. Extensive experience in representing government authorities and landowners in complex acquisition cases.

II

Property & Title Disputes

Comprehensive litigation services for property ownership disputes, title verification matters, partition suits, and real estate conflicts. Strategic counsel for safeguarding property rights and resolving complex title issues.

III

Civil Litigation

Robust representation across a broad spectrum of civil matters including contractual disputes, recovery suits, injunctions, and declaratory reliefs. Seasoned advocacy from trial courts through appellate forums.

IV

Criminal Litigation

Vigorous defense and prosecution in criminal matters with unwavering commitment to justice. Experienced representation in serious criminal cases before all levels of judiciary with strategic litigation approach.

V

Government Representation & Advisory

Trusted legal counsel to government bodies, public sector undertakings, and development authorities. Advisory services on statutory compliance, policy implementation, and institutional legal matters.
